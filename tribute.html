Here's a time line of Gates's life:

  • 1955 - Born in Seattle, Washington
  • 1968 - Enrolled in the private Lakeside prep school,and he wrote his first software program there.
  • 1973 - Entered Harvard University.
  • 1975 - Started Micro-soft with Paul Allen, who is his friends.
  • 1976 - Changed company name to Microsoft .
  • 1985 - Launched Windows 1.0.
  • 1995 - Launched Windows 95.
  • 2000 - Established the foundation with Melinda Gates.
  • 2001 - The Original XBOX released.
  • 2002 - The foundation accomplished efforts to help install 47,000 computers in 11,000 libraries in all 50 Stares.
  • 2006 - Helped launch TerraPower, a company which objects to serve the world with a more affordable, secure, and environmentally friendly form of nuclear energy.
  • 2008 - Left his daily job at Microsoft.
  • 2014 - Tried to make a viral video with Jimmy Fallon.
  • 2017 - Teamed up with Roger Federer in the fourth annual Match for Africa, a charity tennis match.
  • 2019 - Joined long-time friend Warren Buffett to serve customers at Dairy Queen, a company which produces Soft Cream and Fast Food. However, It did not go well.
  • 2021 - Published his book How to Avoid a Climate Disaster.
